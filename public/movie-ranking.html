Best movies according to Shruti

My top three bollywood movies

Rockstar

This movie features Ranbir Kapoor,who is my all time favourite.
College student Janardhan is a simpleton who desperately seeks inspiration for the musician inside him. Although heartbreak helps him reach his goal, it also leads him to self-destruction.

Dear zindagi

this movie feaures Alia Bhat and Shahrukh Khan.
After a series of career and relationship downturns, Kaira begins suffering from insomnia. She contacts Dr Jehangir Khan, a psychologist, who uses unconventional methods to treat her.

Three Idiots

In college, Farhan and Rajat bond with Rancho due to his refreshing outlook. Years later, a bet gives them a chance to look for their long-lost friend whose existence seems rather elusive.
